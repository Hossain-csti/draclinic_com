ABOUT US

Core Focus & Values

As an integrated and LCP certified healthcare centre, Dr. A Clinic provides various treatments in : General Medicine, Aesthetic Medicine, Skin Diseases, General Wellness, Regenerative Medicine, and Women’s Rejuvenation.


We emphasise on quality treatment, with utmost honesty and attentive service according to patients’ needs. By holding onto our aesthetic values strongly, we only perform the latest US FDA approved treatments with top-notch laser equipments, slimming programmes, and other skincare treatments.

Teamwork

We believe that team work is the key to providing the best services possible. Our Medical Doctors and Therapists work closely with each other to provide the best experience for our patients

Professional

We gather only the best doctors and therapists with exceptional skills and experience in the Medical Aesthetic Industry to help achieve your aesthetic goals or relieve symptoms of skin diseases with satisfied outcome.

Premium Quality

Patient's and customer satisfaction is our top priority. We make sure to deliver the best treatments with effective results, using only US FDA and KKM approved Medical-Grade Devices.

DR. ARIEL CHANG

MEDICAL DIRECTOR

M.D. (Russia)
LCP Board Certified
PGDip Dermatology (Cardiff University ,UK )
Accredited by American Association of Aesthetic Medicine AAAM (USA)

Dr. Ariel Chang is the founder and Principal of Dr. A Clinic.As a LCP-certified and fully registered Aesthetic Physician with Ministry of Health, Dr. Ariel Chang has over 10 years of experience in medical settings, with special interest in Dermatology and Ophthalmology at government hospitals before embarking on the journey of Aesthetic Medicine. She has conducted numerous eye procedures and eye surgeries for patients, while mastering ocular and facial anatomy over the years.

While discovering special interest in Dermatology and Aesthetic Medicine, Dr. Ariel Chang was awarded Post Graduate Diploma in Dermatology from Cardiff University in UK with outstanding performance, and was subsequently nominated into enrolment of MSc. in Dermatology by Cardiff University. Dr. Ariel is also an active member with Academy of Family Physicians of Malaysia (AFPM), AAAM, ISD, KCCS and ISSCR.

As an active member in various international associations and college, Dr. Ariel Chang constantly keeps herself updated by participating in international congress, workshops and trainings.

Dr. Ariel Chang is also a strong believer in Stem Cell Therapy for Healthy Aging, Disease Prevention and General Wellness. Dr. Chloe obtained her Doctor of Medicine (MD) qualification from Russian National Research Medical University. Dr. Chloe is a down-to-earth doctor with great enthusiasm and patience for her work. It is Dr. Chloe’s greatest mission is to help people regain self-confidence and enjoy a higher quality of life.
